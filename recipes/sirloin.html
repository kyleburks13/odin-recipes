Sirloin Recipe

Sauteed Sirloin Description

Pull raw sirloin out of fridge and let sit for 20 minutes. Season evenly with salt, pepper, and garlic powder on both sides. Rub in seasoning. Mash up garlic cloves and have 1/2 cup of sliced butter ready. Turn stove to high. Put in tbsp olive oil and wait for it to smoke. Throw on steak and let sear for 60 seconds then flip. Do same on this side now then flip again. Turn every minute for 6-8 minutes. Throw in mashed garlic and sliced butter. Grab spoon, tilt pan and baste the steaks with the butter. Off once desired temperature of meat. Medium rare recommended. Let steaks sit for 10 minutes before cutting into.

Ingredients

  • Sirloins
  • Garlic cloves
  • 1/2 butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Season steaks
  2. Olive oil in
  3. Sear both sides for 60 seconds on high heat
  4. Flip every 60 seconds for 6-8 minutes total
  5. Baste with butter and garlic cloves after 3 minutes
  6. Serve and enjoy
